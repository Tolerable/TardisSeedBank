Interdimensional Transit Information

At Tardis Seed Bank, we're committed to delivering your genetic time capsules safely across all temporal zones and dimensions. Our shipping policies are designed to ensure your cosmic seeds arrive securely, no matter where or when you are in the universe.

1. Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer several shipping options to accommodate different timeline needs:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Time Stream 7-10 business days Free for orders over $50 ($5.99 otherwise)
Accelerated Quantum Delivery 3-5 business days $8.99
Temporal Express 1-2 business days $15.99

Transit times are estimates and may vary based on your dimensional coordinates, temporal anomalies, or planetary weather conditions.

2. Stealth Packaging

All orders are shipped in discrete, unmarked packaging to protect your privacy. Our advanced temporal cloaking technology ensures your package remains undetectable to primitive scanning systems, ensuring safe arrival at your timeline.

3. Shipping Restrictions

Due to interdimensional regulations, we cannot ship to certain blocked timelines or parallel universes where cannabis genetics are prohibited. It is the customer's responsibility to ensure they are legally able to receive seeds in their dimension or timeline before placing an order.

4. Tracking Information

Tracking numbers will be provided for all Accelerated and Express shipments via email once your order has been processed. Standard shipments receive basic timeline monitoring but not full quantum tracking capabilities.

5. International & Interplanetary Shipping

We ship to most Earth timelines and compatible dimensions. Additional shipping charges may apply for interplanetary destinations or particularly distant timelines. Please note that customs or temporal border inspections may delay international deliveries.

Important Note: Some countries have strict policies regarding the importation of seeds. Please research your local laws before ordering.

6. Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During high-volume periods or cosmic storms, processing may take slightly longer.

7. Missing or Delayed Packages

If your package experiences a temporal displacement or does not arrive within the estimated delivery window, please contact us within 30 days of the expected delivery date. We will investigate timestream anomalies and work to locate your order or process a replacement.

8. Shipping Insurance

All orders include basic temporal insurance against standard shipping damage. For full quantum-state protection covering all possible timeline aberrations, additional insurance is available at checkout for 5% of your order total.

9. Contact Our Time Agents

If you have questions about shipping or need to report a temporal anomaly with your delivery, please contact our shipping department at TardisGenetics@gmail.com with "SHIPPING" in the subject line.
